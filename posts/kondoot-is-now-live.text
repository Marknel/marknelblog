# Kondoot is now live!

 - published: 2011-05-11 17:18
 - tags: web
 - link: http://kondoot.com

After a great deal of work we are now live!

[Kondoot is a new social network](http://kondoot.com) centred around live video. You can broadcast yourself, watch other people's broadcasts, or just text/video chat with your friends. We are excited about launching and are working on adding all sorts of fun new features ;-)
